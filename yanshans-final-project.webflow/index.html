Help parents and their kids engage in enjoyable interaction and better communication

Background
There are 17 million +Millennial women in the U.S. have become new mother, At different stages of baby development, mothers face different challenges. After six months, the challenge shifts to effectively communicating and engaging emotionally with baby.
Research Approach
In order to understand what specific problems they face, we conduct 1 on 1 interview with 6 months
Who Do We Design For

Problem Statement
How might we craft a product or service that help parents, who are tired after work, to feel energised and look forward to having a relaxing, joyful, and effective interaction with their 24-30 month-old babies, thereby reducing the pressure and time required for learning to interact, making the process enjoyable, while also supporting the baby’s effective cognitive development in recognising shapes and colors?

Concept Solution
EmoConnect Pal …which can customise activities that take care of new mother's current emotional state, allowing her to play with ease and feel relaxing,energized and fun, reducing time required for learning to interact, while also supporting baby’s effective cognitive development, Thus enhancing emotional communication between moms and children
Benefit 1

Customised Engaging Activities to Care for Moms' Emotions

Tailor moms’ engaging activities based on their baby's info and their preferences Help moms relax by enhancing their positive feelings through sharing today's emotions. EmoConnect Pal will customise today suitable activities for baby's development and mom's emotions Capture mom’s life happiness moment with baby, and review mom and baby's "Emotional Diary" to effortlessly revisit cherished life moments.

Benefit 2
Certainty about Suitable Activities with 10-second Tutorial Videos
Learn with ease by a 10-second tutorial video to simplify learning and reduce preparation time.
Benefit 3

Boosting Moms' Satisfaction through Community-Shared, Self-created Activities

Enhance mothers' sense of achievement and make the experience more engaging by allowing them to create and share their activities with other mothers when they feel inspired Moms can see the “Activities Ranking” and how many times the activities they created were used by other parents. By playing activities and uploading activities to earn points, moms can redeem gift packages needed for their children's next development stage

Successful Stories
Real stories obtained from mothers
One cozy evening, Lauren follows the 'Today activity' from EmoConnect Pal to watch rocket launch on TV with her family, after informing EmoConnect Pal that she's feeling a bit tired today.
As a rocket launch appeared on TV, Lauren’s husband noticed his child's eyes light up with excitement. Seizing the moment, he quickly fetched the LEGO rocket set they had purchased earlier. They started building the LEGO rocket, piece by piece together.
Lauren’s husband  taught about rocket parts and their functions, transforming a fun activity into a learning experience. This enriching session strengthened their family bond, making it a cherished and memorable moment of joy and discovery for everyone.
Cindy initially used coloured building blocks to teach her baby about different colours. 
Later, she noticed the colourful vegetables and fruits in her kitchen and linked them with the blocks to teach her baby their colors, shapes, and names. 
The baby was thrilled, and Cindy felt a great sense of accomplishment.  She successfully engaged her baby in learning through interest, and receiving positive feedback. So she wants to share her idea to inspire other mothers in EmoConnect Pal too.
Final Solution

Setup Preference Challenges

Choose “Activity” cards and the AI assistant will customize activity preferences based on your baby's information and your preferences, offering tailored guidance for fun interactions.

2. Daily challenge based on Your Mood and Preference
Select your mood and the AI assistant will recommend "Today's Activity" for engaging with your baby, including a 10-second tutorial video to simplify learning and reduce preparation time.

3. Emotion Diary

System will capture your life happiness moment with your baby, and you could review your baby's "Emotional Diary" to effortlessly revisit cherished life moments.

4. Share Your Self Created Challenges to Community
Moms can create and share their activities to other moms in the system when they have inspirations.

5. Self-Created Challenges Ranking

Moms can see the “Activities Ranking” and how many times the activities they created were used by other parents.

6. Earn and redeem points for children's growth packages
By playing activities and uploading activities to earn points, moms can redeem gift packages needed for their children's next development stage.
